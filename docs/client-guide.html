Getting Access

You don't need a username or password. You access your dashboard with a unique Access Key provided by your project manager.

Open the Client Portal

Go to https://client.varunaat.in in any browser.

Enter your Access Key

Paste the key your project manager shared with you (it looks like a long code, e.g. a1b2c3d4e5f6...).

Click "Access Dashboard"

Your project list loads. If you have multiple projects, they all appear here.

Select a Project

Choose from the dropdown to view its dashboard.

🔑

Keep your access key private. Anyone with this key can view your project data. If it is compromised, ask your project manager to rotate it — a new key will be issued immediately.

💡

If you see "Server is warming up — retrying automatically", just wait 30–60 seconds. The backend is starting up and the app will retry automatically.

Progress Charts

Daily Activity Bar Chart

Shows how many towers were captured and uploaded each day. Useful for spotting high-productivity days, slow periods, or days when no work was done.

Days marked as non-working (e.g. due to rain or access issues) are highlighted so you know why there's a gap.

Cumulative Progress Line Chart

Shows the total captured and uploaded towers over time, as two lines. As the project progresses, both lines climb towards the total tower count. The gap between the two lines shows how much data has been captured but not yet uploaded.

Completion Forecast

The Prediction Box calculates estimated completion dates based on the current pace of work. It uses the average daily capture and upload rates observed so far.

Issues & Non-Working Days

Tower Issues

When a pilot encounters a problem with a tower (access denied, equipment issue, tower needs replacement), it is logged as an Issue. The Issues panel shows:

ColumnMeaning
Tower No.Which tower has the issue

Non-Working Days

Days when no field work was possible are listed here with the reason. Common reasons:

  • Heavy rain / bad weather
  • Access denied by authorities
  • Equipment failure
  • Public holidays

These days are also marked on the Daily Activity chart so you can see exactly why there are gaps.

FAQ

How often does the data update?

The dashboard auto-refreshes every 30 seconds silently in the background. You don't need to reload the page.

Can I share my dashboard with a colleague?

Yes — share your access key and the URL client.varunaat.in with them. They paste the key and see the same dashboard.

My access key stopped working

Your project manager may have rotated the key. Contact them for the new key.

Why does the upload % lag behind capture %?

Data capture (drone flying) happens in the field. Data upload (processing and sending) happens later, sometimes the same day or the next day. A gap between the two is completely normal.

What does "Acquisition: Complete" mean?

It means all towers in the project have been captured — the drone has flown over every tower. Upload processing may still be ongoing.

I see fewer towers on the map than the total count

Only towers with GPS coordinates (from the KML file) appear on the map. If some towers are missing, ask your project manager to check if the KML file covers all towers.
